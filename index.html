Are you a Computing student in NUS? Or do you want to find out what School of Computing is about?

NUS Students' Computing Club presents Computing Day 2016, a one day event to celebrate our presence in the Computing World as one School of Computing (SoC) family. We extend a warm welcome to anyone who is keen to know more about us. With technology-related booths to fun-filled games, everyone can definitely find something that would catch their interests. So what are you waiting for? Take this opportunity to relax and join us in an exciting adventure of modernity!
